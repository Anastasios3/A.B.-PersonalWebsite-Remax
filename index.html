The Ellinikon: Redefining Urban Living in Athens

Experience Europe's most ambitious urban regeneration project, creating a smart, sustainable, and luxurious coastal city within Athens.

  • Smart City
  • Sustainable Living
  • Luxury Residences

The Ellinikon is transforming the former Athens International Airport into a visionary coastal metropolis. Spanning an impressive 6.2 million square meters, this LAMDA Development project offers:

  • A stunning 2 million sq.m. Metropolitan Park - set to become Europe's largest coastal park
  • 3.5 km of revitalized coastline featuring a 1 km public beach
  • State-of-the-art smart city infrastructure for efficient and sustainable living
  • World-class amenities including shopping destinations, cultural venues, and sports facilities
  • A diverse range of real estate options, from luxury apartments to exclusive seaside villas

As your dedicated ReMax agent specializing in The Ellinikon, I'm here to guide you through the exceptional residential offerings in this groundbreaking development.

Little Athens: A Vibrant New Neighborhood

Little Athens is the heart of The Ellinikon's residential offerings, featuring over 1,000 modern homes designed by renowned local and international architects. This exciting new neighborhood combines the best of urban living with the tranquility of coastal life.

Key Features of Little Athens:

  • Diverse range of residences, from stylish apartments to family homes
  • Prime location with proximity to The Ellinikon Park and beaches
  • Vibrant community with a variety of retail, dining, and wellness facilities
  • Sustainable design principles and smart home technologies
  • Excellent connectivity to other parts of Athens

Signature Developments in Little Athens:

  1. Park Rise: 88 luxurious residences designed by BIG (Bjarke Ingels Group), offering panoramic park and sea views
  2. Pavilion Terraces: An iconic complex with organic building formations and direct access to the main pedestrian street
  3. Promenade Heights: Nine circular and elliptical buildings surrounded by lush green spaces
  4. Atrium Gardens: A striking complex with easy access to Little Athens' amenities
  5. Trinity Gardens: Three buildings offering tranquil living on the edge of The Ellinikon

Riviera Tower: A New Icon on the Athens Skyline

The Riviera Tower, designed by the world-renowned Foster + Partners, is set to become Greece's first "green" residential high-rise and a true architectural landmark of The Ellinikon.

  • Architectural Marvel

    200-meter tall structure with a distinctive design that maximizes views and natural light

  • Sustainable Living

    Cutting-edge green technologies and biophilic design principles for eco-friendly luxury

  • Luxurious Residences

    Over 150 premium apartments with breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea and Athens

  • World-Class Amenities

    Includes a spa, fitness center, pool, and concierge services for residents

The Riviera Tower represents the pinnacle of luxury living in The Ellinikon, offering residents an unparalleled urban lifestyle with the serenity of coastal views. Each apartment is designed to the highest standards of comfort and sophistication, featuring smart home technologies and sustainable materials.
